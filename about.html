About

What is FMGG?

Fancy Mouse Genetics Guide is a collection of genetic information relevant to fancy (AKA hobby) mouse breeders. It's a Facebook group, several charts, and a collection of written information that will eventually be a book. Most of the genetic information is compiled from cited outside research rather than being observed by the author.

Who is it by?

I, Ashe R Orsak, am responsible for all of the work. I've spent many hours researching, drawing emojis, making charts, developing websites, and writing information for others to use. All of my content has always been free to use, share, and remix non-commercially with attribution (credit). See the footer for more information on licensing.
